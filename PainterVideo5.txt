In Painter baking textures seems to be a pre work set up that you will want to do so that you know your
textures are good and ready. Baking textures will be applied to your additional maps, the given texture
maps.Baking is one, if not the first thing you will do when you get your project started. When baking, 
the virtual envelope should not be parallel with the mesh, having it offset will make it continuous.
However, finding out the bestpossible outcome, boils down to trail and error. Too low, it becomes flat,
unrealistic shading, and the mesh starts to miss details.Too high, and there are oddities and purtrutions.
